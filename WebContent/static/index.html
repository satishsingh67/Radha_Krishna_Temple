ABOUT TEMPLE

Shri Radha Krishna temple is situated in the east direction around 200 mtrs from sahlaur bazar . This temple is under construction and slated to be ready by 2025. The temple place is known as Radhe nager from the day of bhumi pujan which was scheduled on 23rd November 2023. The land which was donated to shiv mandir Sahlaur by our ancestors, has been selected for Shri Radha Krishna temple construction with the consent of our villagers .
